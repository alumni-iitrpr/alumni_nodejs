Alumni Student Mentorship Program

ASMP is a platform for tapping in the multitude of experience and knowledge of the Alumni for the benefit of the students and Alumni alike. As distinguished IIT Ropar graduates, Alumni hold a wealth of information and knowledge that current students can benefit from. Their expertise and advice are more valuable to students wanting to sail a similar kind of boat ranging from MBA (India, Abroad), MS, various kinds of jobs, civil services, entrepreneurship, etc.

For Alumni, please email alumni@iitrpr.ac.in, to express your interest. A suitable mentee will be assigned to you based on closest match between your profile and experiences with a student’s aspirations and profile.

Hangout with Alumni

Following the overwhelming response during the meeting in November, the need for a regular Alumni-Student interaction session was realised. Alumni association aims to bridge the gap with “Hangout with Alumni”.

In this, we have Alumni speak about various topics based on your responses. To raise a request please email alumni@iitrpr.ac.in.

We have had multiple sessions on

  • General discussion on what people do after IIT?
  • MS/PhD Abroad Info Session
  • MBA Info Session

Graduate School Application Review

The time of year when many students are in the process of applying to or researching for graduate programs in universities abroad can’t be more taxing. Choosing the right university, the right courses, fund arrangements, etc. all need careful deliberation. The where, when, why and what, all seem to be very important.

The application process is intensive but if planned out carefully, one can reach their best place to pursue their desired higher education course. Applicants seek a lot of advice from people who have gone through this phase and it is chaotic since students may not know who to contact for what.

In order to make the most out of the experience of graduates from IIT Ropar who are studying (or have studied) at foreign universities, we aim to achieve

  • Every applicant gets fair attention with as much relevant guidance as possible.
  • Not only a few graduates pursuing higher studies get bombarded with assistance requests but everyone gets to be a mentor.

How this works?

  1. Interested aspiring students should email alumni@iitrpr.ac.in - those who have appeared for the GRE or have it scheduled in the next 3 months shall be considered preferably. Fall aspirants of upcoming academic year would be taken on priority.
  2. We will match the applicants with mentors who have volunteered to help.
  3. Applicants and mentors continue the discussions/interactions as per their convenience.

It will also help alumni, who want to pursue MS/PhD after some years of work-experience.

Alumni Book Donation Program

It is not easy to deny that an institute's success is also based on the strong foundation of its vibrant library. Keeping this in mind, we believe as Alumni of IIT Ropar we can contribute in our own small way by donating a book. The book can be from any genre and be donated by following the steps given below -

  • Fill the Preliminary Book Donation Form
  • Once the request to donate a book is accepted by the Library, you will get an email from them stating the same. That email will also contain the link to form number 2 which will ask for details regarding the way you would like to donate (via e-commerce sites, personal donation etc.)

Please fill in this form at the earliest as well. It is suggested that you fill your IIT Ropar email id in these forms.

At the end of the day, in one way or another, we are all in the business of sharing of knowledge and hence, it would be nice if we could give something back via a book.

Wishing you all continuous success and hoping that we continue to become better together.
